Home

Smart metering for residential properties, giving homeowners real-time visibility into electricity and water usage with seamless prepaid control.

Our meters support prepaid and postpaid configurations, integrating with mobile top-up platforms so households can recharge anytime. Tamper alerts and remote disconnection keep utilities in control.

Commercial

Scalable metering for offices, retail centres, and multi-tenant buildings, reducing revenue leakage and enabling precise cost allocation across tenants.

Our sub-metering systems let property managers bill each tenant accurately. Real-time dashboards and automated reports eliminate manual meter reading entirely.

Industrial

Heavy-duty metering for factories, mines, and large-scale sites, with full integration into existing SCADA and ERP platforms.

Our meters handle high-load three-phase environments and support demand management to avoid peak-tariff penalties, feeding data directly into your operations infrastructure.
